#SMS : Shutri

The decentralized pipeline bridging independent research with machine-led synthesis and multi-platform broadcasting.

How It Works

1

Submit Your Rabbit Hole Phase 01

Submit your article, blog post, thread, or research paper for free. We are actively looking for collaborative intelligence.

Provide your publication link, name, and email in the portal. It costs nothing. We review submissions that showcase deep logical rigor, mathematical analysis, or unique perspective shifts.

2

deepMind Ingestion & review mempool

If accepted, the research is ingested into the deepDive deepMind database and published under the Mempool queue.

We process your content using our Google NotebookLM Deep Mind. We formulate a synthesized research note—an academic review and critique, not a verbatim copy—and host it in the Mempool. You will receive an immediate email notification with your draft's status.

3

Media Synthesis & Feedback template

Reviewers produce custom podcasts and animations, moving the note to the Template queue for a 3-day review window.

An internal reviewer evaluates the note. If aligned, they generate:

  • An **audio podcast** using custom NotebookLM voices.
  • Visual **infographics and animations** using Mosaic.

We notify you via email and provide 3 days to evaluate the synthesis. If you do not approve, we take it down immediately. Podcast audio is shared on Spotify, Apple, and Fountain.fm. Visuals are broadcast on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube to collect public commentary.

4

Consensus & Blockchain Minting chain

Public interaction is moderated. After 3 days, finalized research is minted into the deepDive blockchain ledger.

The assigned reviewer engages with, clarifies, and answers public comments. After the 3-day review period, once 21 successfully reviewed articles are aggregated, they are minted into the permanent **deepDive block** and published on our core platform under Creative Commons.
